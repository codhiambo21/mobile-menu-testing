OUR "CARE" PLAN

C- - Compassion

We uphold each other through the physical, emotional, spiritual and ethical journeys that define the human experience no matter what disabilities we may have.

A - Accountability

We pledge to be responsible for the human and financial resources entrusted to us by our members and their families.

R - Respect

We affirm the dignity and diversity of all members striving to always honor the needs, talents and hopes of each member we serve.

E - Excellence

We commit ourselves to curiosity. innovation, advocacy and leadership in the field of supporting the developmentally delayed.
